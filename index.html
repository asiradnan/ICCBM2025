2nd International Case Conference on Business and Management (ICCBM) 2025

AI, Innovation & Sustainability: Rethinking Enterprise, Practice & Leadership
📅 Conference Date: December 18, 2025 (Thursday)
📍 Venue: BRAC University, Merul Badda, Dhaka - 1212

About the Conference

The rapid advancement of AI and innovative digital technologies is reshaping the landscape of enterprise, leadership and professional practice across the Global South. As these economies navigate industrial transformation, the integration of AI-driven innovation, digital and green business models, and sustainable or circular practices becomes essential for long-term economic growth. Through the unique case-based approach, this conference explores how emerging innovative technologies can enhance business efficiency, drive sustainable development and redefine leadership paradigms in regions often constrained by resource limitations and structural inequalities. The overall goal of the conference is to provide a dynamic forum for academics, practitioners, policymakers, students, and other stakeholders to explore and exchange knowledge on the intersection of AI, innovation and sustainability. Through case studies and insightful discussions, the conference will examine the evolving landscape of enterprise, practice, and leadership in the Global South, driving innovative solutions for sustainable development and digital growth.
